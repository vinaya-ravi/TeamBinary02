INFJ - Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging

Advocates are idealistic, insightful people driven by their values and imagination. They seek depth in relationships and are loyal friends. Advocates have high expectations of authenticity. As parents, they aim to shape independent, ethical children but can be rigid.

Career-wise, Advocates seek purpose, often in helping roles like teaching, counseling, and healthcare. They thrive creatively as writers or artists. But rigid environments stifle them. At work, Advocates want to collaborate and help others. They dislike criticism.

As managers, Advocates empower teams and identify strengths. But they have little tolerance for unethical behavior. In summary, Advocates are committed to their ideals and to helping others. But they should balance high standards with flexibility.
